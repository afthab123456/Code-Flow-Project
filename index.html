Welcome to Royal College

185 years and counting

From its inception at the verandah of a modest church with a mere 20 pupils to becoming the largest and most prominent educational institution in Sri Lanka, the Royal College has a documented history of nearly two centuries and counting. Through war and glory, through trial and triumph, the pillars of the College that stood strong tell a story like no other.
