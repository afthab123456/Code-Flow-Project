Covid-19 Vaccination Program for Grade 11 Students

This notice concerns all grade 11 students and A/L students who missed the initial vaccination program.

The college has arranged a COVID-19 vaccination program on the 13th of November 2021 (Saturday) from 7.30 a.m. to 01.30 p.m.

All students should enter the school via the Primary Gate. It is compulsory for all grade 11 students and ’23 A/L students (students below the age of 18) to bring their Student Record Book and a letter of consent for vaccination signed by their parents/guardians. A/L students of ’21 and ’22 are required to bring only their Student Record Book. All students should come in school uniform, clean shaven and with a proper haircut. We advise students to have their breakfast prior to coming to school.
